Movement Studies - Lost In Translation

Lost in Translation is a training program designed to bridge the gap between the movement we execute and the meaning that often gets overlooked.

By intertwining physical practice with theoretical exploration, this program focuses on the cultural, anatomical and contextual elements that inform and shape movement - aspects that are often “lost in translation” during conventional training.

The program is designed to enhance the understanding of how cultural and contextual factors influence movement, ensuring that participants not only perform movements but also grasp their deeper significance.
